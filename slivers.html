Operational

Detection and analysis

Compares actual service delivery against agreement terms. Detects scope creep (providing unpaid services) and coverage gaps (agreements that don't match reality).

Layer 4

Validation

Multi-model consensus

Reviewer:Amount

Billing Math

Validates calculations. Hours x Rate = Amount. Verifies rates match agreement terms. Checks quantity gaps are accurate.

SAFETY ARCHITECTURE

Tiered Trust Model

Slivers operate in tiers based on risk. Higher tiers require human approval.

0

Autonomous

Read-only operations. Sensors, Resolver, Detectors, Reviewers. Cannot cause harm. Run continuously without approval.

2

HIL-Gated

Internal write operations. Invoice creation, agreement updates. Requires human approval. Every action logged.

3

External

Operations affecting external systems. Syncing to accounting, sending invoices. Requires explicit additional approval.
